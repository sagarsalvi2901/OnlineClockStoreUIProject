MaxCDN is org that provide content delivery network using its servers.
BootstrapCDN was founded in 2012 by David Henzel and Justin Dorfman at MaxCDN.
BootstrapCDN is also a public content delivery network. can load CSS, 
JavaScript, from its servers.

The WebP format is a new image format from Google which employs both lossy (a trade-off between quality and size) 
as well as lossless (reduction in size with no compromise in quality) compression techniques.
WebP lossless images are 26% smaller in size compared to PNGs. 
WebP lossy images are 25-34% smaller than comparable JPEG images.
